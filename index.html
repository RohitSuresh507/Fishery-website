About Fishin India

Our Story

The Fishin’ India Private Limited is the largest importer of tilapia in the world and one of the largest importers of frozen fish, with offices and processing sites located around the world.

Established in 2018, our minority-owned company was built on the principles of supplying the highest quality products, at the lowest prices possible, while providing an unmatchable level of customer service. We focus every day on building strong, long-lasting partnerships with our customers, and we have the distinct honor of continuing to supply many of our original customers.

Today, Fishin’ India Private Limited supplies many of the largest retailers and food services companies in the world. We offer both private label and Fishin’ branded packaging options.Our newest brand, Rising Tide, made its debut in retail stores throughout the country in early 2017.The Rising Tide brand includes both commodity and Value-Added items.

Our vertical integration and long-term supplier partnerships allow us to provide specific retail packaging, custom products, and bulk offerings. In addition, The Fishin’ Company provides a wide range of sustainably-sourced, wild-caught and farm-raised seafood products that are MSC and BAP certified, including species such as tilapia, salmon, cod, swai, haddock, snow crab, and mussels. We pride ourselves on being fully compliant with all food safety and social compliance-related requirements.
